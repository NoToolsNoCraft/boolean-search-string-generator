What is Boolean Search String Generator?

This app helps recruiters, sourcers, and researchers easily build complex Boolean search strings optimized for LinkedIn. Just enter your job titles, mandatory skills, nice to have skills, locations, industries, and exclusion terms — and the generator will craft a precise search string for you.

Whether you're looking for candidates, job opportunities, business leads, or industry insights on LinkedIn, Boolean logic can give you a powerful edge. This app supports all kinds of searches — from finding niche professionals by combining specific titles and skills, to locating companies in certain regions, or even filtering out irrelevant results. It's perfect for job seekers, recruiters, sales professionals, and market researchers who want more targeted and efficient search results.

💡 Tips for Smarter Searches

      

        New to Boolean search? Start simple by using "OR" to group similar job titles (e.g. "Designer" OR "Creative Director"), "AND" to combine key skills (e.g. "Figma" AND "UX"), and "NOT" to exclude unwanted terms (e.g. NOT "Intern"). The generator ensures the logic is correctly formatted so your results are sharp and relevant.
      

    

  
    

      
🌐 Where Can I Use the Strings?

      

        The Boolean strings you generate here work seamlessly in LinkedIn search, but they're also useful for Google (e.g., "site:linkedin.com/in"), GitHub, job boards, CRM platforms, and even browser extensions that support Boolean logic. It's a versatile tool that fits right into your sourcing workflow.
